How to Play
Goal: eat food, grow longer, build multiplier, and push mode-specific bests.
Classic: walls wrap and runs continue until you hit yourself.
Time Attack: score as much as possible before the timer reaches zero.
Hardcore: walls are solid and every fruit is worth more.
Skins: unlock new looks through score and distance milestones.
Achievements: earn badges from food, distance, modes, multipliers, and lifetime progress.
Controls: desktop uses arrow keys. Mobile can use swipe or on-screen keys from Settings.
